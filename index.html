How it works

Three steps. One tab. Zero copying and pasting.

01 · Predict

Scan a page, get the whole team's emails

Open any LinkedIn company People page. TrailMail reads the names, roles, and company, looks up the verified email pattern with one Hunter.io credit, and predicts an address for up to 15 people at once. Every result is tagged HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW confidence.

  • Anchored to the company's real pattern and verified against multiple records
  • Confidence on every single result, so you know what to trust
02 · Send

Email from your own Gmail, fully personalized

Pick who to reach, write a template once with merge tags like {First}, {Company}, {Role}, and send. Every message goes out one per person from your real account, landing in your own Sent folder.

  • Gmail access is send only, so TrailMail can never read your mailbox
  • No backend, no shared server, no credentials stored anywhere but your browser
03 · Track

See every open and click, right in Gmail

TrailMail badges your Gmail Sent list with ✓ Opened and ↗ Clicked as people engage. Finally, outreach with a feedback loop. You see what is landing without ever leaving your inbox.

  • Tracking works out of the box, with zero setup
  • Respects privacy, and you can self host it for full control
