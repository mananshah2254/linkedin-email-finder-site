LinkedIn Email Finder

Predict work emails on LinkedIn and send them from your own Gmail — no backend.

What it does

  • Scans the LinkedIn profiles on a company People page and predicts each person's likely work email from the company's public email pattern (via your Hunter.io key).
  • Lets you save reusable templates (subject + body) with merge tags like {First}.
  • Optionally connects your own Gmail to send personalized emails to selected people — one message per recipient, straight from your account.
Predictions only. Generated emails are inferred from public company patterns and are not verified — some may bounce. You are responsible for compliant, authorized outreach (consent, identity, unsubscribe).

Privacy

The extension runs entirely in your browser with no server of its own. Read the full Privacy Policy.
